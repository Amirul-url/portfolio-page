Corner detection is a foundational technique within computer vision. It involves identifying salient image points characterized by significant local variations in intensity, effectively pinpointing corners and edges of objects. These detected corner points are highly valuable due to their robustness under various image transformations, including rotation, scaling, and illumination changes. Consequently, they play a critical role in numerous computer vision applications such as image registration, object tracking, and object recognition.
